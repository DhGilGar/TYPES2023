Social event

The social event will take place on Wednesday 14th, and consists of a guided tour around the city center, starting at 6 pm at Torres dels Serrans. To get there, there will be a private bus from the ETSInf after the last session of the day. Please, take notice of the instructions from the organizers during the day.

There are also public transportation options to get to the starting point:

  1. Tram Stop: Pont de Fusta. Line 4.
  2. Bus Stop: Pont dels Serrans. Lines 6, 16, 26, 94 and C2.
  3. Bus Stop: Guadalaviar-Sagunt. Lines 94, 95 and C2.

The end of the guided tour is planned at 8 pm at Nord Station. From there, there is a 30-minute walk or a 5-minute subway ride to the restaurant that offers the social dinner at 9 pm: NH Valencia Center. Please, be there 15 minutes in advance.

To go from the Nord Station to the NH Valencia Center, take the subway at Plaça Espanya, Lines 1 or 2, to Túria.
