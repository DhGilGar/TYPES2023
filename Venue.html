PRACTICAL INFORMATION

Location

The conference and all workshops will take place from Monday June 12th to Saturday June 17th at the ETSinf on Univesitat Politècnica de València.

Public transport in Valencia
  • Subway/tramway and the app.
  • Bus.
  • Bicycle.
  • Airport to Valencia:
    • Subway: There is a metro station on the ground floor of the airport.
    • Bus: There are two lines of bus that go between the airport and the city.
    • Taxi.
  • Transport to the UPV:
    • Tram: lines 4 and 6. Stations: Universitat Politècnica, La Carrasca and Tarongers-Ernest Lluch.
    • Bus:

      Bus Stop: Tarongers-Junt entrada universitats. Lines 93 and 98

      Bus Stop: Tarongers-Facultat de Magisteri. Lines 18, 93 and 98

      Bus Stop: Facultat de Ciències Socials or Campus de Tarongers. Lines 40 and 71

  • Transport to the social act (Torres dels Serrans):
    • Tram: line 4. Station: Pont de Fusta
    • Bus:

      Bus Stop: Pont dels Serrans. Lines 6, 16, 26, 94 and C2.

      Bus Stop: Guadalaviar-Sagunt. Lines 94, 95 and C2.

Museums in Valencia
  • Science museum: Ciutat de les Arts i les Ciències.
  • Fine Arts Museum: Museu de Belles Arts.
  • MUPREVA and ETNO museums (prehistoric and ethnographic).
  • Modern art museum: IVAM.
  • Ceramic National Museum.
  • Museu L'Íber (tin solders museum).
